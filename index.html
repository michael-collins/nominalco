About nominalco

nominalco is a design studio specializing in the development of digital and physical objects, interfaces, and workflows. Our multidisciplinary team applies universal design principles and systemic thinking to solve creative challenges with functional, bold, and appealing artifacts.

We are creative explorers and apply our craft using an elastic design process. Opposed to rigid best practices, we structure a nebulous taxonomy of adaptable design methods into effective workflows. This process includes obsessive analysis of context, constraints, and requirements in pursuit of form, function, and impact. We value creative exploration and experimentation in search of a universal story within each project. We find the way we design to be resilient against bad outcomes.
