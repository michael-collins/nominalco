About nominalco

nominalco is an art and design practice specializing in the development of digital and physical objects, interfaces, and workflows. Our multidisciplinary team applies universal design principles and systemic thinking to solve creative challenges with functional, bold, and appealing artifacts, stories, and experiences.

As creative explorers and apply our craft using an elastic process. Opposed to rigid best practices, we structure a nebulous taxonomy of adaptable design methods into effective workflows. This process includes obsessive analysis of context, constraints, and requirements in pursuit of form, function, and impact. We conduct creative experiments in search of a universal story within each project.

We design images, brands, products, objects, installations, and interactive experiences.
